The Instructor Dashboard

is a new system for instructors to easily identify issues that arise between teams so they can intervene promptly.

The dashboard makes it easy to see which students are contributing to their group project. Instructors can see ratings different students give one another presented in various informative graphs.

A filtering feature lets instructors sort teams by the factors such as most red or most green in order to quickly find problematic or high performing teams.

The team view displays information about the team in a variety of informative displays.

Information about each student is displayed in various views. Instructors can easily get a holistic sense of a team and determine if intervention is needed.

This view makes it easy to identify issues that arise in a team such as social loafing, role ambiguity, and communication breakdowns.

This view shows the ratings this student recieved from their peers.
