Frequently Asked Questions

Have any questions about The Daily Smirk? Check these out.

Students rate their peers every day they work on their project. Instructors can also require that students complete a certain number of evaluations per week.

Student can see the ratings they've received, but feedback is anonymized among group members.

Yes, if a student rates a peer with a dark red or dark green smirk, they'll be prompted to provide more feedback. A series of pre-filled checkboxes are provided, with prompts like: "Did not attend planned group activity/activities" and "Missed the deadline for their contributions". Students are also able to optionally provide their own custom feedback.

Students can access the Daily Smirk through any device with an internet browser. In the future, this may expand to a mobile app.

Yes, students can edit the ratings they've given in the past. Instructors can turn this feature off.

At the moment, instructors manually create groups by assigning students. In the future, integrations with Canvas will be added to easily assign students to groups.

The Instructor Dashboard includes a calendar screen, which shows the different ratings a student gave or recieved. This helpful feature allows instructors to quickly see how students are working with each other over time.
