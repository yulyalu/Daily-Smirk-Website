The Daily Smirk is a novel, smiley-based peer assessment tool for team-based projects. By utilizing high-frequency peer assessment with pre-defined criteria, the Daily Smirk gives students and instuctors real time, actionable feedback about their progress.

How is the Daily Smirk different from other peer evaluation programs?

High-frequency assessment

Students rate their peers any day they work on their group project, allowing them to adjust their performance immediately based on feedback from peers.

Lightweight workload

Completing an assessment is quick and simple. 68% of students said it took less than 1 minute to complete.

Neutral Expectation

A neutral satsifaction (yellow smirk) with peers' contributions is assumed. More specific feedback is only needed for outlier ratings.
